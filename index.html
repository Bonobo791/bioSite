Sahara Matthews is a mother of two, an artist, and a dedicated friend and family member. She attends Hillsborough Community College and spends much of her time studying or working. She's passionate about women's right and does her best to keep her kids in school and successful.

Sahara Matthews enjoys painting art that is emotional and shows how she feels at the given time. She owns a small art studio called, "The Walk of Shame". It's primary purpose is to gain exposure for not only her art, but for the art of many people around Tampa. Her art work has been sold in small galleries around Tampa and in many coffee shops. She aspires to create and do more in the new future as her business grows.
